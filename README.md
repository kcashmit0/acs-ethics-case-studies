# ACS Code of Conduct - Ethical Case Studies

Analysis of real-world ethical dilemmas through the lens of the Australian Computer Society (ACS) Code of Professional Conduct.

## About

This repository explores how ICT professionals should navigate complex ethical situations using the ACS Code as a framework.

## ACS Code Principles

### The Primacy of the Public Interest
- Prioritize public good over personal/organizational gain
- Protect privacy and confidentiality
- Consider environmental and societal impact

### The Enhancement of Quality of Life
- Strive for high quality in processes and products
- Ensure accessibility and inclusivity
- Minimize harm to individuals and society

### Honesty
- Be honest about competence and limitations
- Provide objective professional opinions
- Disclose conflicts of interest

### Competence
- Maintain and develop professional knowledge
- Only undertake work you're qualified for
- Seek assistance when needed

### Professional Development
- Continue learning throughout career
- Share knowledge with colleagues
- Mentor junior professionals

### Professionalism
- Enhance ICT profession reputation
- Respect intellectual property
- Comply with relevant laws and standards

## Learning Goals
- Apply ACS Code to realistic scenarios
- Develop ethical reasoning skills
- Understand professional obligations
- Navigate conflicting principles
